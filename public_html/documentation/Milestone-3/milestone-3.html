Step 1: Choosing Your Pack

The first step in the custom pack experience is looking through our wide array of models to determine which best suits you and your needs.

Step 2: Customizing Your Pack

Here you will be given a 3d model in which you can pick parts, colors, and finishes.

Step 3: Enjoying Your Pack

Finally you will be shown a 3d model of your finished pack. You will still have the ability to go back and make changes. If you are satisfied with your build then you're all set! Now all that is left is for your pack to arrive on your doorstep and for you to start flying!
